Quiet Luxury, Crafted for Life

We are a focused studio creating bespoke kitchens and interior joinery for homeowners, architects, and designers. Every commission is made to order in our County Wicklow workshop using time-honoured craftsmanship and modern digital design tools.

From first sketches to final fit, your project is handled personally. We design with clarity, build from raw materials, spray finishes in-house, and install with care across Wicklow and surrounding areas.

Our Philosophy

Timeless design. True bespoke. Built to endure.

Cove Bespoke exists to create spaces that feel calm, considered, and personal. We believe luxury is quiet confidence: honest materials, refined proportion, and impeccable finishing that will look and feel right for years to come.

There are no catalogue parts. Every cabinet, shelf, and panel is sized to your architecture and made by hand for a precise fit and a lasting result.

A Personal Studio

One maker. One point of contact.

This is a personal service from start to finish. You speak directly with the person who designs, builds, finishes, and installs your project. Communication is clear, decisions are thoughtful, and the craftsmanship is consistent throughout.

We work discreetly and efficiently on site to keep your home calm during installation.

How We Work

From first conversation to final fitting.

  • Discovery
    We begin with a detailed conversation and site review to understand your architecture, lifestyle, and priorities.
  • Design
    Accurate CAD plans and photorealistic visuals let you see proportions, storage, and finishes before anything is built.
  • Craft
    Each component is handmade in our Wicklow workshop from selected timbers and board materials for strength and longevity.
  • Finish
    In-house spray facilities deliver smooth, durable finishes in your chosen colour and sheen.
  • Install
    We deliver and install with precision, ensuring alignments, reveals, and motion are perfect.
  • Made to order for kitchens, wardrobes, media units, wine rooms, and interior joinery
  • Digital design with 3D visuals for clarity and confidence
  • In-house spraying for colour fidelity and consistency
  • Based in Wicklow serving the surrounding areas
