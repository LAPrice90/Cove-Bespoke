About Cove Bespoke

Quiet luxury, crafted for life.

Cove Bespoke was founded on the belief that interiors should feel calm, resolved, and timeless. From our Wicklow workshop, we design and craft kitchens and interiors that balance beauty with everyday ease.

A design ethos built on listening.

Every project begins with careful listening and ends with a space that fits the way you live. We design with clarity, build with care, and finish with precision — creating interiors that feel quietly luxurious.

Handmade in Wicklow.

Our joinery is made entirely to order in our workshop. We work from raw materials, not catalogues — shaping, finishing, and assembling every piece under one roof.

Quality without compromise.

From solid timbers to hand-sprayed finishes, every detail is considered. We combine traditional methods with modern tooling to deliver strength, refinement, and enduring quality.
